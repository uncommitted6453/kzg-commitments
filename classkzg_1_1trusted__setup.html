kzg::trusted_setup::trusted_setup ( int  num_coeff)

Performs the trusted setup step of the KZG commitment scheme.

Computes the group elements G1[s^i] and G2[s^i], up to num_coeff. The maximum degree of a polynomial that can be committed with this setup is num_coeff - 1.

Parameters
num_coeffThe number of group elements to generate (num_coeff > 1)
Exceptions
invalid_argumentif the constraint isn't met
